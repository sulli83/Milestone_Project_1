About Us

Our vision is to deliver inspirational gardens and service that exceed customers' expectations. Daniel Collins established Think Outside in 2007 following his return from extensive travel in Asia, Australia, Central and South America, where he observed aspects of horticulture and landscape design for use in his new venture.
Think Outside is based in Bray, County Wicklow.


Services offered by Think Outside include Consultation, Garden Design and Construction and Maintenance. Projects to date, examples of which are set out below, have included many sizes and shapes of garden, rural and urban areas and common areas. Think Outside has also collaborated with architects and other landscape contractors to bring its experience to bear on individual elements of larger projects.
The extensive portfolio of garden designs demonstrates an ability to work with and enhance a variety of architectural styles whilst creating a unique outdoor space for each client.
