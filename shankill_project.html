Shankill

Desciption

Large Project completed by our team during the winter of 2017. Projects including the contruction of a large Cedar pergola, stone wall and a lot of paving. The project also included retro fitting a large quantity of Old Cobbles Cobbles as retainer on an existing old Tarmacadam driveway. Large specimens we included in this installation were large Carpinus betulus 'Fastigiata' shaped into cones as well as several large Acer species which have matured beautifully.

Project Features

  • Chinese Granite Slabs
  • Portugese Limestone Cobbles
  • Stone Walls
  • Cedar Pergola
  • Old Dublin Cobbles
  • Large Carpinus Specimen Trees
